VISIT ARIOGALA

Enjoy nature and peace in this vibrant small town close to Kaunas city.

Top three activities to do at Ariogala

Visit Babylon as the town locals call it

Babylon is center of Ariogala where you can sit down and enjoy beautiful fountain and everyday life of Ariogal residents. .

Enjoy river of Dubysa

River of Dubysa provide good time not only for swim and other recreational activities but beautiful views.

Check out Ariogalas St.Archangel Michael Church

Build by Grand Duke Vytautas in 1416. It is from a rectangle and it has features of eclectic style. It has two towers.
